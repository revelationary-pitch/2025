Revelationary is a Bible Reading Application like no other

Mission

The Revelationary Project is ultimately intended to help people deepen their understanding of the word of God. The tools featured in the reading application are designed so that a user can discover their own connection to scripture whilst keeping them motivated and engaged in their journey.

Our goal is to improve the reading and study experience a user may have with the Bible whilst establishing a strong and safe community that can engage in online discussions over scripture. Revelationary is more than just a website; it's a movement to inspire and connect believers worldwide.

Chris Palmer – Founder & Developer

Within this meticulously arranged UI ensemble there is a whole host of features to explore and discover. Many of which are new to the Christian community for discussing the Bible online and studying to their own preferences.
Ethical Considerations

Revelationary uses software to encourage consistent Bible study, but we are careful to ensure these tactics remain ethical and non-manipulative. Our goal is to motivate users by rewarding their spiritual growth through features like reading streaks and achievement milestones, fostering a sense of accomplishment without exploiting addictive behaviors. At just $1.99 per month, users gain access to powerful tools like streak tracking and comment functionality, while the Bible text itself will always remain free. Email sign-ups unlock additional features like verse highlighting, ensuring users have meaningful ways to interact with scripture without facing financial barriers. This approach aligns with our mission to create an accessible, spiritually enriching platform without compromising user trust.

Our search API uses a highly advanced search algorithm that enables the user to look up specific chapters, books and verses or how many times a single key word or phrase may appear in the entire Old Testament and New Testament texts. Returning paginated results on the fly.

Making Connections

Every verse can be subsequently linked to a group comments section for that verse. This allows users to share their thoughts, testimonials, ask questions, or engage in discussions with others who are also reading the same verse. This feature is designed to foster a sense of community and encourage users to explore the Bible together, creating a safe space for shared insights and spiritual growth. By connecting users through shared experiences and reflections, Revelationary aims to build a supportive and engaging community that enhances the reading experience and deepens a user's connection to scripture.

A Community Network
Societal Impact

Revelationary’s impact goes beyond individual users — it seeks to create a ripple effect that strengthens communities and nurtures spiritual growth on a broader scale. By making Bible study engaging, accessible, and personal, we aim to inspire deeper connections to scripture, fostering a culture of consistent reflection and meaningful dialogue. Our low-cost subscription model ensures that financial barriers are minimized, while the free Bible text remains universally accessible. The platform also supports a global community of believers, encouraging the sharing of insights, reflections, and questions, creating a space where faith is nurtured collectively. With features like comments, highlights, and streaks, Revelationary promotes lifelong spiritual development, helping individuals grow in their faith while inspiring others to do the same. We believe that by making scripture more interactive, we can contribute to a more spiritually aware and compassionate society.

Revelationary offers a mindful reading streak—designed to inspire consistency, not pressure.

Our verse-by-verse comments section invites users into rich, ongoing Bible discussions—uncovering fresh insights and deeper truths together.

Users can Highlight their favorite Bible verses in the Revelationary app to easily revisit and reflect on them anytime.

Bible Study redefined

With a growing community of believers, Revelationary is not just a Bible app; it's a digital sanctuary that helps users form deeper connections with scripture, with others, and with themselves. By combining modern design, social features, and habit-building mechanics, we aim to make Bible study more accessible, engaging, and rewarding than ever before. Our platform is designed to be inclusive, accessible, and welcoming to all — regardless of denomination, background, or level of Bible knowledge.
